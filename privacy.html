1. What we collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Roblox account identifiers: such as your Roblox user ID and username (used to display your profile and link balances).
  • Public profile details: such as an avatar/headshot URL (when available).
  • Chat content: messages you send in public chat.
  • Support messages: if you contact us, we may keep the message and related metadata to help resolve the issue.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device & browser data: approximate device type, browser, OS, and settings used to render the app.
  • Log & security data: IP address, timestamps, and event logs needed to detect abuse and troubleshoot outages.
  • Gameplay telemetry: game actions and results (bets, multipliers, outcomes) used to operate games and produce live feeds/history.

1.3 Payment / transaction data

If you use deposit/withdrawal features, transactions may be processed by third‑party providers or platform mechanics. We may store transaction identifiers, amounts, timestamps, and status for auditing, fraud prevention, and support.

2. How we use information

  • Operate the Service: run games, keep accounts in sync, and display balances and history.
  • Safety & fraud prevention: detect bots/exploits, prevent abuse, enforce rate limits, and investigate suspicious activity.
  • Support: respond to requests and fix issues.
  • Analytics: understand performance and reliability to improve the Service.

3. When we share information

We may share information in limited situations:

  • Service providers: hosting, database, logging/monitoring, and payment/transaction providers required to run the Service.
  • Legal compliance: if required by law, lawful requests, or to protect rights and safety.
  • Business changes: if the Service is transferred (merger/acquisition), information may transfer as part of operations.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Cookies & tracking

We may use cookies or similar storage (including localStorage) to keep you logged in, remember preferences, and restore interrupted sessions. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not work correctly if cookies/storage are disabled.

5. Social / platform logins

RoBet can interact with third‑party platforms (for example, Roblox APIs for usernames/avatars or platform mechanics for certain deposits/withdrawals). We only use data we receive for operating the Service and support.

6. Retention

We keep information only as long as needed to operate the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and prevent fraud. Some logs may be retained longer for security and auditing.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no online system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your devices and accounts secure.

8. Minors

RoBet is intended for users who are 18+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a minor has provided us data, contact support and we will take appropriate action.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To request this, contact support via Discord (linked in the site footer) and include your Roblox user ID.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact support via our Discord community (linked in the footer of the site).
